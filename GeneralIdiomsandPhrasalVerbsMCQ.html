About JeeAI Tech

Purpose of JeeAI Tech

JeeAI Tech aims to provide a platform for individuals to practice and enhance their knowledge through multiple-choice questions (MCQs). It covers a wide range of subjects and topics, making it suitable for students, employees, and professionals looking to improve their skills or prepare for interviews.

How JeeAI Tech Works

Each topic on JeeAI Tech contains 10-15 questions. Users can answer these questions one by one and submit their answers at the end. Once submitted, the system calculates the user's score, displays the percentage, and provides explanations for the correct answers.

Features of JeeAI Tech

  • Wide range of topics across various subjects.
  • Detailed explanations for each question.
  • Score calculation with percentage results.
  • Option to review answered and skipped questions.
  • Responsive and user-friendly design.
